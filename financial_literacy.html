Quick Financial Tips

1

Pay Yourself First

Set aside savings before paying any bills. Aim for at least 20% of your income.

2

Emergency Fund

Build an emergency fund covering 3-6 months of living expenses for unexpected situations.

3

Track Your Spending

Monitor where your money goes. Use apps or spreadsheets to track every expense.

4

Avoid Lifestyle Inflation

When you earn more, resist the urge to spend more. Increase your savings instead.

5

Diversify Investments

Don't put all eggs in one basket. Spread investments across different asset classes.

6

Regular Reviews

Review your financial goals and progress monthly. Adjust strategies as needed.
